The People's Liberation Army at the Founding of New China

  A truly mighty division, armed with rifles and flying the flag of 81, The People's Liberation Army (PLA) marches forward vigorously and vigorously. Create a new China with one hand.


The People's Liberation Army
at the Early Stage of New China

  Rapidly developed and artillery and communication forces of navy, air force and army were established. Technical forces such as engineers and railways have formed operational capabilities.


China air force

  The Air Force of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, in the War of Resistance Against the United States and Assistance to the DPRK, followed the principle of "training from actual combat". The way to grow up in battle is from not fighting air battles to learning to fight air battles, from being able to fight small-scale air battles to being able to fight large-scale air battles. As the volunteer air force is generally at a disadvantage, it also pays a high price.


The Chinese Navy.

  The emergence of the volunteer Navy in the Korean battlefield is a confrontation stage in the battlefield situation. In May 1952, the Navy decided to send torpedo boat troops, mine troops and shore artillery troops to the DPRK to participate in the defense of the West coast.

Chinese Artillery

  The Chinese People's Volunteer Army (PLA) is a group of soldiers who carry out combat tasks with firepower in the PLA sequence. Artillery is divided into ground artillery and antiaircraft artillery according to its combat mission. Volunteer artillery, usually ground artillery. It is the backbone of the volunteer army's fire assault on ground targets in the war of resisting US aggression and aiding Korea.

Chinese Railway Soldier

  During the War of Resistance Against the United States and Aid Korea, the railway troops cooperated with other brotherly forces. A large number of railway engineering support tasks have been accomplished, and a steel transportation line that "can not be broken and bombed continuously" has been built, which ensures the transportation on the railway.

Chinese Engineers

  In the War of Resistance Against the United States and Aid Korea, volunteer military engineers were "all for the front line." Under the ideological guidance of "all for victory", more than 2,200 kilometers of new roads were built, and more than 9,600 kilometers of roads were repaired and maintained. More than 1,100 new bridges, with a total length of 63,000 meters, and more than 285,000 cubic meters of earth and stone for tunnels are built. More than 16,000 command posts, personnel shelters and shelters were constructed, and more than 63,000 landmines and time bombs were cleared. More than 1.2 million square meters of airport and 54,000 warehouses, hospital wards and barracks were built.

Chinese Communications Corps

  The Chinese People's Volunteer Army (PLA) serves as a communication support force in its ranks. In October 1950, the leading organs of the Volunteer Army consisted of nine short-wave radio stations and a telephone team (equivalent to company); each army had various types of correspondents. There are 2500 - 3000 personnel (5% - 6% of the total number of the army), more than 60 radio communication machines (including walkie talkies), cable bills, etc. There are more than 370 switchboards, covering more than 440 single kilometers.
