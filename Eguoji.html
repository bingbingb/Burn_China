Chinese Silk Culture

  Silk was first used in Central Asia, then in Western Europe, and finally in Britain. Silk is used for the daily wear, writing and painting materials of princes and nobles, and for decorating religious relics. Knights wear silk under lock armor to improve their comfort.

Four Inventions
  Before the emergence of modern European civilization, China's four great inventions were introduced into the West one after another, which exerted a certain influence on the development of science and technology in the West. The emergence of printing has changed the situation that only monks can read and receive higher education, and facilitated the spread of culture. The use of gunpowder and firearms destroyed the ideological shackles of medieval Catholicism in Europe. The compass reached European navigators. It made it possible for them to discover America and sail around the world, laying the foundation for the development of World Trade and workshop handicraft industry in the West. Four great inventions have left a brilliant page in the history of human science and culture. These great inventions have influenced and benefited the whole world. It has promoted the progress of human history.
