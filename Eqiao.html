Development of Bridges

  Nowadays, there is a saying in the world bridge circle: Looking at Europe and America before the 1970s for the construction of world bridges, Look at Japan in the 1990s and China in the 21st century. In 2016, just last September, The achievements of bridge construction in China have attracted more and more attention from the world.

  China retains ancient bridges with a long history like Zhaozhou Bridge. They are also building world record-breaking highways. The construction of new railway bridges, expressways and high-speed railway bridges is particularly noticeable. Statistics show that in the past 10 years, the average number of highway bridges in China has increased by 27,000 seats per year. By the end of 2015, the number of highway bridges in China reached 779,000, making it the largest bridge country in the world.
