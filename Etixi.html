Military Law System

  In December 1952, the People's Revolutionary Military Commission of the Central People's Government and the State Council jointly promulgated it. Provisional Regulations of the People's Republic of China on Militia Organizations, adopted by the Second Session of the First National People's Congress in July 1955 The Military Service Law of the People's Republic of China. In the same year, the People's Liberation Army implemented three systems: salary system, rank system and compulsory military service system. Since the 1990s, the construction of military laws and regulations has been obviously accelerated. The Law of the People's Republic of China on the Protection of Military Facilities has been promulgated successively. The Civil Air Defense Law of the People's Republic of China, the National Defense Law of the People's Republic of China, the National Defense Education Law of the People's Republic of China, Active Service Officers Law of the People's Republic of China and Reserve Officers Law of the People's Republic of China, etc. By the end of 2005, The National Defense Law of the People's Republic of China has been the leading force in the formation of 15 laws specializing in the regulation of national defense and military construction, as well as decisions on relevant legal issues. There are 181 military regulations, 88 military administrative regulations and more than 3000 military regulations in the legal system.


Perfecting the System of Military Laws and Regulations with Chinese Characteristics

  　Since the Nineteenth National Congress of the Communist Party of China, under the strong leadership of President Xi and the Central Military Commission, military legislation has been reformed and improved. Institutional mechanism, implementation of key legislative projects, and formulation of a number of military laws and regulations urgently needed for reform and preparation for war The promulgation or revision and implementation of military laws and regulations with Chinese characteristics continue to develop and improve the system.


Persist in Leading Military Legislation with the Target of Strong Army

  The Party's goal of strengthening the army in the new era is to lead the army's construction, reform and preparations for military struggle. In the new era, our Party's general strategy of building up the army and administering the army is the general plan. Insisting on examining and leading military legislation with the goal of strengthening the army, It reflects the distinct requirements of the times for military legislation, and points out the key tasks and direction for the construction of the system of military laws and regulations.


Grasp the Key to Improving the Quality of Legislation

  "If good law is established in the world, the world will be governed; if good law is established in one country, the country will be governed." The report of the Nineteenth National Congress of the Communist Party of China points out that: We should promote scientific legislation, democratic legislation and legislation in accordance with the law so as to promote development and ensure good governance with good laws. Chairman Xi stressed that: We should improve the legislative mechanism, standardize the legislative authority, strengthen the top-level design of legislation, and improve the quality of legislation to a new level.


Speeding up Military Legislation in Key Areas

  If our army wants to fulfill its duty of maintaining world peace and regional stability, it can not do without legal follow-up guarantee. We should speed up the formulation and improvement of laws and regulations concerning the operation and management of overseas support bases and the use of overseas military forces.
