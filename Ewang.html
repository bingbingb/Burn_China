  Crossing China's energy arteries, And here is the longest natural gas transmission project in the world. The World's Largest Water Conservancy Project

  The world's largest water project delivers water. It has already been equivalent to carrying 770 West Lakes from south to north, benefiting more than 100 million people. Reducing groundwater exploitation by 800 million cubic meters, Beijing's groundwater has rebounded for the first time in 16 years. China's grand idea of water resources allocation from north to South has finally come true.
