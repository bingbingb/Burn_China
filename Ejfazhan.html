 Construction of Military Talents

  Since the 21st century, the Central Military Commission and its headquarters have promulgated a series of policies and regulations and taken measures to vigorously strengthen the construction of military personnel. Focusing on building five contingents of commanders, staff officers, scientists, technical experts and sergeants, To train a large number of high-quality new military personnel who are suitable for information construction and competent for combat tasks under the condition of information technology.

Qualities that Soldiers Should Have

  With the development of military technology and weaponry, the number of military professional and technical posts has increased to thousands. It takes several years or even more to train a complex professional and technical post. Generally speaking, knowledge war needs five kinds of military talents.

Scientific and Technological Literacy

Qualities that Soldiers Should Have

  Knowledge war is a war based on the use of information and information weapons. The right to control information has become the dominant right to grasp the initiative and win the war. There are more than 1500 computers on an American aircraft carrier. A modified B-52 strategic bomber has hundreds of computers. Therefore, in the comprehensive quality of new military talents, The most important thing should be the information quality with information knowledge and information technology as the core.

Information literacy

Qualities that Soldiers Should Have

  Knowledge war is a highly innovative war. The CPU, the core component of the computer, is updated almost every 18 months. Only by constantly breaking the pattern of the previous war, making full use of new technologies and innovating means of warfare can soldiers in knowledge warfare win the war.

Innovative Literacy

Qualities that Soldiers Should Have

  Knowledge war is a war of all dimensions. Operational operations are carried out in three dimensions: land, sea, air, sky, electricity, network and psychology. Multidimensional super space is launched at the same time. Strategies, battles, tactical actions and political, diplomatic and military struggles are highly integrated. Combat forces are highly integrated and systematic, so new military personnel, especially commanders, are required to have a strong strategic concept and the ability to control the overall situation.

Strategic literacy

Qualities that Soldiers Should Have

  No matter how knowledgeable war is, its essence is confrontation by means of violence. The danger of war, Complexity and contingency are more important than any other social activity. Therefore, the new type of military personnel must have rich experience in military practice. A soldier without knowledge and practical experience can't win a battle.
